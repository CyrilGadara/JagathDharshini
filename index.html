Our Mission

Jagath Dharshini Public Charitable trust is a registered society, headquarter in Jangaon serving vulnerable children in 12 mandals of Jangaon District. As an operational organization, Jagath Dharshini Public Charitable Trust works alongside community-based organizations, government and non-government organizations to enable vulnerable children irrespective of caste, creed, color, and gender to reap the benefits of India's policies.
